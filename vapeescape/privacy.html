VapeEscape Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

This privacy policy outlines how VapeEscape ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our practices.

What Information We Collect

When you use VapeEscape, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name (required for authentication and syncing)
  • Usage Data: Puff counts, timestamps, daily limits, streaks, quit plans, and goals
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, and crash reports (for diagnostic and analytics purposes)

We do not collect:

  • Payment or credit card information (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Precise location data
  • Health data from Apple HealthKit
  • Advertising identifiers

Subscriptions and Payments

VapeEscape offers optional premium subscriptions to unlock additional features. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.

Auto-Renewal: Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your Apple ID account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

Free Trial: If a free trial is offered (currently 3 days), any unused portion will be forfeited when you purchase a subscription.

Managing Subscriptions: You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide core functionality such as data tracking and analytics
  • Sync your data securely across devices via Firebase
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience
  • Send optional motivational notifications and reminders (with your permission)
  • Send promotional emails and progress reports (you can opt out anytime)
  • Provide customer support

Third-Party Services

VapeEscape uses the following third-party services to provide and improve our service:

  • Firebase (Google): For secure data storage, authentication, and cloud sync. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics: To understand app usage patterns and improve user experience. Analytics Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To collect crash reports and diagnose issues. Crashlytics Terms
  • Apple Sign In: For secure authentication. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign In: For secure authentication. Google Privacy Policy

These services are compliant with international privacy standards including GDPR and CCPA. We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs.

How We Protect Your Data

Your personal data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and authentication practices. We use Firebase's secure infrastructure which is SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account:

  • Your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes
  • Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of optional data processing
  • Lodge Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at freeway995@gmail.com or delete your account directly within the app (Settings → Delete User Profile).

Cookies and Tracking

VapeEscape does not use cookies for advertising or behavioral tracking. We respect your privacy and honor "Do Not Track" browser settings.

Age Restrictions

VapeEscape is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you are under 18, please do not use this app or provide any information to us. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us to request deletion.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States (where Firebase servers are located). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
