VapeEscape Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how VapeEscape ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our practices.

What Information We Collect

When you use VapeEscape, we may collect the following information:

  • Email address (required for authentication and syncing)
  • Usage data (such as puff count, streaks, and goals)
  • Device-related metadata (for diagnostic purposes)

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide core functionality such as data tracking and analytics
  • Sync your data securely across devices
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Send optional motivational messages or reminders

How We Protect Your Data

Your personal data is stored securely and never sold or shared with third parties. We use authentication and encryption best practices to protect your information. VapeEscape does not collect payment data or store sensitive personal identifiers beyond your login credentials.

Cookies and Tracking

VapeEscape does not use cookies for advertising or behavioral tracking. We respect your privacy and comply with “Do Not Track” browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or analytics tools that access your personal data. Any essential services (e.g., authentication providers) are compliant with international privacy standards (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

Children’s Privacy

VapeEscape does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us to request deletion.
